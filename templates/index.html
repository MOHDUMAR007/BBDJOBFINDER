Introduction

BBDJobFinder is a platform designed to enhance the placement process for final-year students at BBD. In today's competitive job market, BBDJobFinder offers a solution by connecting final-year students with alumni and companies. Alumni can browse resumes uploaded by the placement department and offer opportunities, increasing students' chances of securing jobs and internships.

About Us

BBDJobFinder is a platform aimed at enhancing the placement process for final year students through alumni-driven support. We enable the placement department to upload student resumes and allow alumni to browse and refer candidates directly to companies.

By leveraging the alumni network, we aim to bridge the gap between education and employment, giving students a better shot at achieving their career goals.

Features

  • Authentication System: Ensures secure access for all users.
  • Alumni Profile: Connects former students with current placement activities.
  • Placement Cell Dashboard: Centralized management for placement teams.
  • Resume Manager & Reference System: Streamlined resume handling and referrals.
  • Verification System: Validates user data and documents.
  • Feedback Manager: Gathers insights to improve services.
  • Admin Dashboard: Enables monitoring and admin control.
  • Search and Filtering: Quickly find suitable profiles.
  • UI Manager: Delivers a consistent user experience.
