Privacy Policy

At BBD JobFinder, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, contact details, educational background, resume/CV, and other information you provide during registration or profile updates.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our platform, including pages visited, features used, and interactions with other users.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies for analytics and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account on BBD JobFinder.
  • To facilitate job applications, resume uploads, and communication between students, alumni, companies, and placement officers.
  • To improve our services, personalize your experience, and provide relevant job recommendations.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform.
  • To contact you with important updates, notifications, or support information.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We share your information only with authorized users such as placement officers, alumni, and company representatives for recruitment purposes.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of BBD JobFinder and its users.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by logging into your account.
  • You may request the removal of your account and associated data by contacting us at owner@bbdjobfinder.com.
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and improve our services. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page. Your continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at owner@bbdjobfinder.com.
